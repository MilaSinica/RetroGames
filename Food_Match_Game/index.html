Help

Test your memory and concentration by finding the matching pairs. You have 30 secons to remove all cards from the board. The aim of the game is to try to memorize the location of different cards. Choose two cards and turn them face up. If selected cards match - they dissapear. If they do not match they are turned face down again. Clear all cards before the timer runs out. Select EASY game mode to play with 12 cards and HARD mode to play with 18 cards. Select NEW GAME to start game over.

Good Luck!
