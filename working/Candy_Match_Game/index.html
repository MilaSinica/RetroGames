Help

Test your reaction and attention with this match lines online game. You have 60 seconds to find as many matching candy combinations as you can. Click one by one 2 candies you want to swap and match sets of 3 or more candies. Note, only candies next to each other can be swaped. When matched, the candies will dissappear and will be replaced by new ones. Match 3 candies to get 5 point, 4 candies to get 15 points and 5 candies for 25 points. Your goal is to get 200 points score before the time runs out. Be watchful and fast - this will not be easy. HINT: try to make longer combinations to gain more points faster.

Good Luck!
