The game has 2 players, playing in rounds. In each turn, a player rolls 2 dices as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score. BUT, if the either die roll is 1, all player ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn. FYI: the probability of rolling a 1 is 31% or 11/36 and the probability of rolling a double is 17% or 6/36. If both dice show the same result then the player gets double score. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn. The first player to reach 30 points on GLOBAL score wins the game. Enter your own preffered score instead of 30 points to change GOAL SCORE value.

Good Luck!
