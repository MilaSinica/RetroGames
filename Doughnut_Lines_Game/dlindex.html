Help

Move doughnuts from cell to cell to group them into the lines of the same color. However, after each of your moves the computer drops three more doughnuts onto the board. To avoid filling up the board you should gather the doughnuts into lines of 5 or more. When such a line is complete, the doughnuts are removed from the field and your score grows. The new doughnuts will not be added to the field after a line removal. The goal of the game is to stay in game as long as possible. The game ends once the whole game board becomes filled up with doughnuts. Hint: the more doughnuts you remove at once the greater your score is.

Good Luck!
