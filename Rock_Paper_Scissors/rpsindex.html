The game is an expansion on the game Rock, Paper, Scissors. Rock crushes scissors and smashes lizard. Paper covers rock and disproves Spock. Scissors cuts paper and decapitates lizard. Lizard eats paper and poisons Spock. Spock vaporizes rock and smashes scissors. Earn 5 points by beating computer in 5 rounds to win the game. Press "New Game" to begin a new fight.
