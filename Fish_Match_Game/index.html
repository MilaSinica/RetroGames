Help

Test your reaction and attention with this match lines online game. You have 60 seconds to find as many matching combinations as you can. Click one by one 2 pictures you want to swap and match sets of 3 or more pictures. Note, only pictures next to each other can be swaped. When matched, the pictures will dissappear and will be replaced by new ones. Match 4 or 5 pictures to get more points. Your goal is to get 500 points score before the time runs out. Be watchful and fast - this will not be easy. HINT: try to make longer combinations to gain more points faster.

Good Luck!
